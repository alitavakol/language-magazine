Introduction

Hi, everybody and welcome to the third issue of the Hot English Magazine – the fun magazine for learning English.

Saint-Exupéry published his most famous book, The Little Prince (شازده کوچولو), in 1943. The story is a commentary on the different thought processes that exist between children and adults. Download the issue, and learn about Saint-Exupéry, his book, and his sad ending.

Of course, that’s not all and we’ve lots more fun things for you to read and listen to so you can learn lots of useful English. We’ll be looking at functional language on “making suggestions”, stone idioms, expressions about driving in the city, and a lot more!

If you are a beginner-level or intermediate-level student, this issue is for you. There are:

10 Beginner Lessons
6 Intermediate Lessons
1 Upper-Intermediate Lesson
