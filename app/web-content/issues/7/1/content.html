Home Alone

Great grandmother has luxury block of 30 flats to herself.

“When I bought the apartment in the large building I had no idea it would just be me”, said Mavis Ward, 83. Six months ago, Mavis bought an apartment in a block of flats in Cornwall. She thought she would have neighbours. But when she moved in, she realised all the other flats were only holiday apartments. So Mavis has the building and its facilities to herself for most of the year. “Now I’m a Lady of the Manor,” said Mavis. “I really can do as I please. I’ve got a gym with a swimming pool, a treadmill, a rowing machine, an exercise bike and a sauna.” The pensioner added, “When the owners do come back and use their flats at the weekends, they come and say hello. I think people see me as one of the fittings of the place – I’m like a permanent feature.” ✪
