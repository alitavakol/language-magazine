Devilled Eggs

Learn a quick and easy way to make devilled eggs.

Ingredients

  1. ¼ cup mayonnaise
  2. 6 eggs
  3. 1 tsp mustard
  4. Spring onion
  5. Relish (optional)
  6. Paprika

Steps

  • Boil the eggs until they’re hardboiled.
  • Try to remove the shells without breaking up the eggs.
  • Cut the eggs lengthwise.
  • Take out the egg yolk and place it into a large bowl.
  • Use a fork to break up the egg yolk until it’s like breadcrumbs.
  • Add the mayonnaise, the mustard and some relish (optional).
  • Rinse the egg whites under water then pat them dry.
  • Place the egg yolk mixture into a Ziploc bag and squeeze it into the egg whites. You could also use a fork or spoon to do this.
  • Sprinkle some paprika and little pieces of chopped spring onion on top.

Enjoy! ✪
