Hi, everybody and welcome to another issue of the Hot English Magazine – the fun magazine for learning English.

This month, we’re looking at 12 useful phrases to improve your spoken English. They’ll help you sound more natural, fluent and native!

Of course, that’s not all and we’ve lots of more fun things for you to read and listen to so you can learn lots of useful English. You will improve with a lot of Read & Listen articles, a travel English topic on “the emergency”, insect idioms, a vocabulary clinic on “neogotating” skills, and a lot more!

If you are a beginner-level or intermediate-level student, this issue is for you. There are:

8 Beginner Lessons
6 Intermediate Lessons
