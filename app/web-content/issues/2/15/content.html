Hostel Booking

❶ Listening I

Who says it? Receptionist or Caller? Listen once and check.

  1. "There will be four of us."
  2. "How much is each room?"
  3. "I just need your credit card details."
  4. "What is the cardholder's name, please?"

❷ Listening II

What is the total cost of the hotel booking? Listen again to check.

  1. €500
  2. €600
  3. €700

❸ Listening III

Listen again and answer the questions.

  1. How many people are there in the party?
  2. How many double rooms do they want?
  3. When are they arriving?
  4. When are they leaving?
  5. How much is each room?
  6. What is the cardholder’s name, please?
  7. What is the credit card number?
  8. What is the credit card expiry date?
