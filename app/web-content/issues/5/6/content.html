Asking someone to guess

  • What is it?
  • What do you think it is?
  • What do you reckon it is?
  • Have a feel of it.
  • Smell it.
  • Go on, have a guess!
  • You’re close! Warm! Warmer! Bingo! Correct!

Functional Conversation: The Guess

Dave: So, what do you think it is?
Sally: I’m not sure.
It looks like a book.
Dave: Try again.
Sally: Well, it feels like a CD or a DVD.
Dave: Closer.
Sally: Is it a DVD?
Dave: Bingo!
